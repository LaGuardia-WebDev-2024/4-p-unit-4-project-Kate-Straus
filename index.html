Fetus

  • "Fetus Harry" is the early stages of his career, specifically when he was a teenager and newly joining The X Factor UK in 2010.

Frat Boy

  • "Frat Harry" is a fan fav era.
  • Around 2012-2013, during the early One Direction years. The first tour.

Bandana

  • Fans often said "he won't take that damn bandana out"
  • During the second tour.

Long Hair

  • Lots of suits and boots this era but still rocking the skinny jean.
  • This era came after Zayn left 1D and is my favorite era
  • It also sparked the famous Harry at Louis' Moms wedding photo (middle)

Dunkirk

  • Harry starred in the movie 'Dunkirk' his first acting gig.
  • He had also freshly split with 1D which called for a haircut
  • It also sparked the famous Harry holding his hair photo that broke the internet (left)

Prince Hair

  • Harry releases his first solo work, self titled.
  • This sparked the now known Prince Hair era, which was more than hair, it was wearing cool suits and heeled boots.

My Policeman

  • Harry stars in a movie called 'My Policeman'
  • This sparks the clean hair look

HSLOT

  • Harry Styles Love on Tour era, one of the most fun eras to be a Harry fan
  • Funky outfits and short hair, more grown out than Policeman era though. Harry starts sporting scruff?

The Buzzcut.

  • Speculation for weeks after HSLOT ended started rising that Harry buzzed off his hair
  • After weeks of speculating and photos of a his buzzcut taken by fan from really far away, he confirmed it in a now iconic post (left).
